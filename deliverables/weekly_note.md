# 📊 INDMoney Weekly Pulse - Weekly Note

**Date Range:** Jan 29 – Mar 24, 2026
**Total Reviews:** 105

## 🏷️ Top Themes

1. **Technical Issues and Glitches (39 reviews, ★1.7)**
   Users report frequent app crashes, frozen screens during critical trading moments, inability to exit positions, incorrect order execution, and data syncing problems. These issues often lead to significant financial losses and frustration, with some users mentioning UI rendering bugs and outdated charts.

2. **Lack of Features and Functionality (24 reviews, ★2.5)**
   Users are seeking additional features such as flexible loan payment options, extended transaction history, improved watchlist management, broader global market access (beyond US/India), advanced analytical tools (e.g., XIRR, fundamental data), commodity segment trading, and easier account closure processes. Some report removal of previously available functionalities.

3. **Poor Customer Support (19 reviews, ★1.0)**
   This theme highlights critical dissatisfaction with customer support. Users complain about unresponsiveness, missed callbacks (despite promised short wait times), tickets being closed without resolution, evasive email responses, and general ineffectiveness in addressing urgent financial issues or losses caused by app malfunctions.

4. **Positive Experiences and Appreciation (18 reviews, ★4.9)**
   Users highly appreciate the app's comprehensive financial features, including net worth tracking, US and Indian stock investments, and mutual funds. Many praise its user-friendly interface for beginners, faster performance after updates, and responsive design team that actively gathers and implements feedback.

## 🚀 Suggested Actions

* **Enhance Core App Stability and Performance:** The highest volume of negative reviews (39, avg. 1.7) centers on critical technical issues, app crashes, and trade execution failures. Prioritizing robust engineering to eliminate these glitches is crucial.
* **Overhaul Customer Support System:** With the lowest average rating (1.0), poor customer support is severely damaging user confidence. Implement a system for timely callbacks, ensure all tickets are resolved effectively.
* **Prioritize Key Feature & UX Development:** Users are actively requesting essential financial management features (e.g., XIRR, comprehensive transaction history, more payment options) and improved UX for existing functionalities.
